How it works

When companies or the government hold information about you, you have the right to get a copy of it. We help you in the process.
1

Send a written request

Use this site to find the organisation and approach them by email or letter.

2

Respond to organisation

The organisation will be in touch with you to check your identity.

3

Leave feedback

Come back to the site and leave a record of what happened. This will help others make successful requests.
